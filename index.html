The Goal: Support client’s vision every step of the way

VORM Architects created this visionary project in the very heart of Rotterdam.And we’re proud to say we supported them through the whole process withour visuals. From winning the architectural competition, to marketing thisluxury real estate to potential clients. Our journey together goes for years now.And each stage called for visualizations focusing on different features of thismagnificent design.

The Value: Metropolitan spirit of The Lumiere Tower

Lumiere Tower is a place for global citizens to call their home. We focused on painting a picture of this unique environment for living and working. Our visuals show luxury apartments, office spaces, shops and facilities where residents can experience art, culture and social events. And on top of that (quite literally) green roof terraces and balconies.

The Secret Ingredient: Form

The outstanding architecture of Lumiere skyscraper speaks volumes. So wehad no choice but to make it the center of attention. And to showcase itstop-quality materials, high ceilings, full-glass facades, lots of greenery both invast outdoor spaces and intimate roof terraces.

Volta, EST

The Goal: Showcase a new life of historic factory

We’ve crafted a wide set of visual content for this 1912 industrial building,turned into luxury townhouses and commercial spaces. At each stage of thereconstruction development, we assisted our Estonian client withvisualizations, animations, apartment selectors and aerials

The Value: Homage to industrial architecture

Volta taps into traditional features of industrial buildings to create loftsexaggerating the sense of light and space. We focused on showing that in ourvisualizations. Both in shots of comfortable bright interiors and luxuriousexteriors with an exceptional landscape.

The Secret Ingredient: Form

Unique architecture of revitalized historic buildings deserved a spotlight. Sowe made sure our visual content gives justice to its multi-level apartmentlayouts, soaring double-height ceilings, dramatic 5-meter-high archedwindows and private green terraces.

"Vivid Vision has been a long-term visualisation partner for the extensive mixed-use development at Volta Quarter. This project is challenging in all aspects, requiring the continuous integration of new 3D models into an ever-evolving construction setting. Vivid Vision has produced both exterior and interior visuals, as well as animations, for up to nine buildings simultaneously, while also managing a master model comprising over 30 buildings. This task demands perseverance and strong character to maintain both enthusiasm and high production quality. I am confident in our ability to produce world-class images together with Vivid Vision and grateful to have you by my side!"
